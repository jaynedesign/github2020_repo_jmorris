Wash your hands whenever they are visibly soiled or after you have been in contact with body fluids or contaminated surfaces.
Step 1. 5-Seconds. Wet your hands and apply soap.
Step 2. 20-Seconds. Scrub the palms of your hands. Then scrub the back of your hands. Use back and forth motion.
Step 3. 20-seconds. Scrub between fingers. Scrub palms with finger tips. Do this on both hands.
Step 4. 10-Seconds. Scrub each thumb using palm and fingers of other hand. Use twisting motion.
Step 5. 5-Seconds. Rinse your hands thoroughly.
Step 6. Use a clean single-use towel to dry your hands.
Step 7. Use the same towel as a barrier to turn off the faucet.
Step 8. Your hands are now clean! Repeat these steps any time you come into contact with contaminated surfaces, body fluids, or if your hands are visibly soiled.
